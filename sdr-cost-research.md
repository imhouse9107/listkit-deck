## 6. SDR Ramp Time
- **Bridge Group (2024 report):** 3.1-3.2 months average ramp
- **By segment:** SMB 1-3 months, Mid-market 4-6 months, Enterprise 9-12 months
- **Average tenure:** 16 months total, minus 3.2 months ramp = 12.8 months productive
- **Use in deck:** 3.2 months (Bridge Group)
- **Source:** Bridge Group SDR Metrics & Compensation Report
- **URLs:**
  - https://blog.bridgegroupinc.com/sales-development-metrics

## 7. Percentage of SDRs Who Hit Quota
- **Bridge Group:** 63-68% of SDRs in a given group achieve quota (consistent across years)

- **Use in deck:** Only 63% hit quota (Bridge Group)

## 8. Percentage of SDRs Who Leave Within 12 Months
- **Median annual SDR turnover:** 32% (SOMAmetrics / Bridge Group)
- **12% of companies see annual turnover above 55%**
- **HubSpot data:** Average sales rep turnover is 35%, which is 2.5x the rate of other industries at 13%
- **Average SDR tenure:** 1.5 years (Bridge Group) / 16 months
- **Use in deck:** 35% annual turnover (HubSpot), average tenure just 16 months
- **Source:** HubSpot Sales Blog, Bridge Group, SOMAmetrics, Xactly
- **URLs:**
  - https://blog.hubspot.com/sales/prevent-sdr-burnout
  - https://www.xactlycorp.com/blog/motivation/sales-turnover-statistics
  - https://www.somametrics.com/sdr-attrition-rate/
  - https://blog.bridgegroupinc.com/sdr-attrition-assumption

## 9. Percentage of SDRs Who Leave Within 6 Months

## 10. Cost to Recruit and Train an SDR Replacement
- **SHRM:** Replacing an employee costs 50%-200% of annual salary
- **SHRM average cost-per-hire:** $4,700 (hard costs only)
- **External recruiter fee:** ~20% of first-year salary = ~$10,000-$12,000 on a $55K hire
- **Total replacement cost (all-in):** $30,000-$120,000 per SDR (SHRM formula applied to $60K salary)
- **MarketBetter (2026):** $52,000-$99,000/yr in perpetual replacement cost per seat, factoring pipeline loss + ramp
- **Use in deck:** $10,000-$15,000 direct recruiting cost. $30,000+ total impact per replacement.
- **Source:** SHRM (2025), MarketBetter (2026)
- **URLs:**
  - https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/news/talent-acquisition/real-costs-recruitment
  - https://marketbetter.ai/blog/sdr-turnover-cost-analysis-2026/
  - https://www.joinforma.com/resources/employee-replacement-costs
